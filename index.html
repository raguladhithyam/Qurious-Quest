About the event

Welcome to Qurious Quest, an intercollegiate quiz competition brought to you by Qubate in collaboration with Multiverse Quizzing. This exciting event, set to take place on 18th October, brings together quiz enthusiasts from various colleges to compete in a battle of intellect and wit.




Qurious Quest will also feature top-notch quizmasters, engaging rounds, and exciting prizes for the winners. Whether you're here to win or just enjoy the experience, this event promises a memorable day of learning, competition, and camaraderie.


About Qubate of Kumaraguru


Qubate is the official quizzing and debating club of Kumaraguru College of Technology. The club is dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity and sharpening the minds of its members through a wide range of quiz and debate activities. With a mission to promote critical thinking and public speaking, Qubate regularly organizes events that engage students in healthy competition and collaborative learning.


About Multiverse Quizzing


Multiverse Quizzing (MQ) began its journey in 2016, fueled by a group of passionate quizzing enthusiasts eager to create a platform for sharing knowledge. Since then, MQ has grown into a well-established name in the quizzing circuit, hosting a wide range of quizzes across various categories, from schools to open competitions, along with knowledge sessions designed to stimulate lateral thinking.

Praveenjayaraj (PJ), the founder of Multiverse Quizzing, an experienced quizmaster who has hosted numerous quizzes across India.

Before registering, make sure to read and comply with the following rules and guidelines regarding the event:

Team Composition
  • Teams can only consist of 2 members, and cross-college teams are allowed.
Eligibility
  • Participants must present a valid student ID and their registration confirmation email upon entry to the institution and to the event team.
Registration
  • The registration fee must be paid, and all required details in the form must be completed for participation to be valid.
Quiz Format
  • The quiz includes preliminary written rounds followed by final stage rounds, covering various topics like general knowledge, science, pop culture, and more.
Conduct
  • No electronic devices (phones, laptops) can be used during the quiz. Participants are expected to maintain proper decorum throughout the event.
Time Limits
  • Each round will have a set time limit. Teams failing to submit answers in time will be disqualified from that round.
Decision of Quizmasters
  • The decision of the quizmasters is final and binding on all matters.
Tie-breaker Rules
  • In case of a tie, tie-breaker questions will be conducted. The format will be decided by the quizmasters.
Disqualification
  • Any form of cheating or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.
